Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)

A safe, secure, and respectful campus for all students, faculty, and staff at Bapuji Institute of Hi-Tech Education.

ICC

About ICC

The Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) of Bapuji Institute of Hi-Tech Education (BIHE), Davangere is constituted in accordance with the provisions of the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013. The Committee is committed to providing a safe, secure, inclusive, and respectful environment for all students, faculty, and staff members of the institution.

Objectives

  • To prevent and prohibit sexual harassment within the campus.
  • To promote gender equality, dignity, and mutual respect among all members of the institution.
  • To create awareness regarding the prevention of sexual harassment and related issues.
  • To provide a fair, confidential, and accessible mechanism for addressing complaints.
  • To ensure a safe and conducive environment for learning and professional growth.

Functions of the ICC

  • To receive and address complaints related to sexual harassment.
  • To conduct impartial and confidential inquiries into complaints received.
  • To recommend appropriate corrective and disciplinary measures based on inquiry findings.
  • To organize awareness programs, workshops, seminars, and counseling sessions on gender sensitization and women's rights.
  • To encourage individuals to report grievances without fear of discrimination or retaliation.

Responsibilities

  • Ensure timely and fair redressal of complaints.
  • Maintain confidentiality throughout the complaint and inquiry process.
  • Protect the rights and dignity of all parties involved.
  • Maintain records of complaints and actions taken.
  • Foster a culture of respect, equality, and inclusiveness on campus.

Vision

To create a safe, equitable, and empowering educational environment where every individual at Bapuji Institute of Hi-Tech Education is treated with dignity and respect, free from all forms of harassment and discrimination.

Mission

To uphold the principles of justice, equality, and human dignity by preventing harassment, promoting awareness, ensuring effective grievance redressal, and fostering a culture of mutual respect and responsibility within the institution.

Motto

“Safe Campus, Respectful Community, Equal Opportunities for All.”

Institutional Commitment

Bapuji Institute of Hi-Tech Education (BIHE), Davangere, is committed to maintaining a campus free from harassment, discrimination, and intimidation. The Internal Complaints Committee strives to safeguard the well-being, dignity, and rights of every student and employee, thereby contributing to a positive and productive academic environment.
